What is it?

The kea database is a citizen science initiative from the Kea Conservation Trust to help us keep track of New Zealand's alpine parrot.

How can I help?

If you see kea (banded or unbanded) out and about on your travels, make a note of the details (such as where, when and how many) and report them on the website.

Who's behind this?

A team of dedicated volunteers working for the Kea Conservation Trust.
